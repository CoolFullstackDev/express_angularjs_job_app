Signing Up


  • A new window will open allowing you to create a profile by simply signing up with Facebook® or Google+®.
  • Now you can click 'login' and choose to login with Facebook or Google+.
  • Clicking on the Taskoli icon in the top left corner will direct you back to the home page.

How to Post a Task


  • To post a task, go to the home page and click the "Post a task" icon or click on any of the category options located below and your task will be posted within that category.
  • Fill in all the text boxes on the "Post a task" page. Please give as many details as possible about your task and what you expect to be done. Choose the time and date you wish your task to be completed by. Then go ahead and 'hire' one of the taskers that have made an offer on your task to assign them the task.

How to make an offer on a Task


  • To make an offer on a task you would like to complete, click the 'check it out' button or 'view task' button and then fill out the 'your offer' fields on the right side of the screen. Fill out your offer price and a quick message to the emploter.
  • Then click the "submit" button to make an offer on this task. Please note that you can only make one offer per task.






Asking questions before making an offer on a Task.


  • To ask a question before making an offer on a task, scroll down to the bottom of the 'Task Overview' page. Write your question in the field and click 'Send'.
